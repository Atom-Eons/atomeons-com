# Product and room doctrine

- LifePath is not Life Migration.
- Life Migration is the onboarding / intake / model-building engine.
- Growth and LifePath remain distinct.
- Social is humane coordination, not social-media mimicry.
- Create must preserve artifact-native quality.
- Learn should compress and teach, not imitate dead school software.
- Relax / Zen should reduce chaos, not just decorate it.
- Misfit beta is governed, not random.

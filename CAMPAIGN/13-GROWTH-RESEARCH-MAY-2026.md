Pulled 2026-05-16 to validate/correct the ORANGEBOX 1M-reach plan.

## TikTok 2026 (BIGGEST OPPORTUNITY)

**The reach advantage no other platform has:**
> "A brand-new TikTok account with zero followers can reach 50,000 highly
> targeted viewers on its first post — something that is structurally
> impossible on LinkedIn or Twitter."
> — TokPortal, B2B SaaS TikTok playbook

**Structure that wins:**
- **Hook-Value-CTA** in first 3 seconds
- Stop "brand awareness", start "micro-education" — one question, one person, one answer
- 7-second hyper-focused asset can drive $500K in revenue (Julia Pintar case)
- Submagic hit **$1M ARR in 90 days** via TikTok with affiliate model

**Affiliate model that works:**
- Recruit creators as affiliates
- Pay 30% lifetime commission
- Each creator posts daily
- For ORANGEBOX: $49 × 30% = **$14.70/sale lifetime commission**

## LinkedIn 2026 (validates Q>Q pushback)

**Algorithm shifted:**
- Niche authority over viral reach
- Semantic relevance scoring
- Content shown only to users with demonstrated interest in topic

**Cadence:**
- Month 1: 3 posts/week
- Month 2+: 4-5 posts/week
- **Quality drastically outweighs quantity**

**Format ranking by engagement:**
- Carousels (PDF format): **6.60% engagement** (HIGHEST)
- Native video: ~4%
- Text-only: ~2%
- Single image: ~1.5%

**Conversation-led growth:**
- Comment on prospect's post FIRST
- Then connect
- Then pitch

**Profile vs Page reach:**
- Personal profile: 2.75x more reach than company page
- → Atom McCree personal profile = primary surface, AtomEons company page = secondary

### Promote TikTok to #1 surface
Previous plan ranked LinkedIn as the highest-reach lever. Reality: TikTok's
50K organic ceiling on first post + viral lottery makes it THE play for

### Convert LinkedIn posts to carousels
The L1/L2/L3 paste-ready content in CAMPAIGN/11 — convert to 6-slide PDF
carousels for the 6.60% engagement rate. ~3x conversion lift vs text post.

### Launch affiliate program

### 7-second cuts

Already doing this with bcherny / 49agents / MaxRovensky / SnazzyLabs /

viral AI threads — NOT 100K spam comments.

## What we should NOT do (from research)

- ❌ "1,000 bad leads" — quote directly: "is a waste of your sales team's time"
- ❌ LinkedIn CPCs at $12-18/click — organic > paid for indie SaaS
- ❌ Brand-awareness video — micro-education converts
- ❌ Cold DMs without prior engagement
- ❌ Generic templates — semantic relevance scoring catches this

## Compound flywheel target

Day 1-3: ship 5 reels, watch which gets traction
Day 4-7: double down on the winning angle, post 2 more in that style daily
Day 8-14: recruit 3 affiliates from people who engaged with winning reel,
         convert the angle into LinkedIn carousel + YT Short

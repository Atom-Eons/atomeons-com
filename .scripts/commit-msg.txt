FRESHNESS POLICY (in the code comment)

  Numbers are hand-coded so they never silently go stale. An honest
  stat block treats freshness as part of the claim — the audit date
  is in the eyebrow, the counts are reproducible, and the next ship
  rebakes them. No live DB calls; no slow first-paint; no "30 days
  ago" lies hiding in CMS state.
